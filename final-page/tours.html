Tours that we recommend you

Cozumel Shore Excursion: Underground River and Cave Swimming Tour

Description

Leave the crowds behind and explore Yucatan's extensive underwater river system on this stress-free shore excursion from Cozumel, ideal for nature lovers and first-time visitors. After a jungle walk exploring the caves, immerse yourself in freshwater cenotes and swim among stalagmites and stalactites. Learn about the geological history of the region and hear stories about the ancient Mayan civilization.

Snorkeling to El Cielo by Private Boat

Description

If you've always wanted to learn how to dive, Cozumel is a great place to do it. This three-hour private course is ideal for enthusiastic beginners who are not yet ready to get the diving certification. You'll learn all the skills you need to start diving safely with your PADI-certified instructor. It even completes an underwater dive to gain experience and confidence.

Atlantis submarine expedition, Cozumel

Description

Immerse yourself in the crystal clear waters of the Caribbean in a state-of-the-art submarine on an underwater excursion from Cozumel. Dive to depths of 30 meters (100 feet) to discover the marine magnificence of Chankanaab Protected Park. Your journey on the Atlantis submarine will take you to the heart of Cozumel's most colorful and captivating coral reef; marvel at the treasures of tropical fish as your knowledgeable guide provides you with interesting information about the wildlife
